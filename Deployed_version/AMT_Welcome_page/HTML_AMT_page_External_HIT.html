This is a scientific survey about human memory when visualizing videos. To collect data, we designed a "memory game" that is proposed in this HIT.

Because we need time to measure long-term memory, the experiment is split in two parts:

  • Part 1: in which you are about to participate, will last less than 25 minutes;
  • Part 2: which you must perform only 24 to 72 hours after part 1, will last less than 20 minutes.
Both parts are mandatory, and you will be payed only after participating to part 2. Please note that no email will be sent to remember that you have to perform the second part. To access part 1 and part 2, please follow the SURVEY LINK.

 

During the whole experiment, you will need three elements to access the two parts:

  • Your ID as an Amazon worker (recalled below if you accept the HIT)
  • The password to access Part 2 of the experiment, which will be given to you at the end of Part 1
  • The password which will be given to you at the end of Part 2, and which will have to be pasted in the box below, in order to be payed.
Please be sure to note all this information in a safe place.

 

Requirements:

  • Use Chrome or Firefox to perform the task
  • At minimum, ADSL internet connection required (because of the videos)

 

Please note that your attention will be monitored through the experiment by some 'very-easy-to-recognize' videos, and if you do not recognize most of them, you will not be allowed to continue with the second part of the experiment.

You will be returned your performance at the end of the experiment. Please just pay attention and enjoy this interesting memory game!
